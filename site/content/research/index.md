# Research Archive

Deep-dive market research, technical analysis, and competitive intelligence for the
Sponge Stamps project -- a custom stamp manufacturing venture targeting the construction,
decorative concrete, and DIY markets.

This archive contains 17 research documents spanning competitive landscape analysis,
material science, customer segmentation, patent review, and distribution strategy.
